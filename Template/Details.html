These books are so much fun! At the bottom of the page they’ll give you a choice like, if you want to follow Jimmy into the scary cave, turn to page 31. If you want to tell him never mind and go to your grandma’s house because you heard she’s baking cookies, turn to page 25. They lead you down a different trail with every choice you make, and that makes reading the same book again and again decidedly NOT boring!
